To be, or not to be, that is the question:
Whether it is nobler in the mind to suffer
The slings and arrows of outrageous fortune,
Or to take arms against a sea of troubles.

The robot learned a small language model from symbols.
It watched characters, guessed the next one, and improved.
Attention looked backward through the sequence.
Generation began with a tiny prompt and continued step by step.

In a small laboratory, data becomes pattern.
Pattern becomes prediction, and prediction becomes text.
